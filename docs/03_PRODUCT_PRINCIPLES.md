# Product Principles

- Red Flag > Policy
- Evidence > Opinion
- Human Review > AI
- Simple > Smart
- Ship > Perfect
- SEO > Virality
- Community Signals > Community Posts
- Solo Founder > Enterprise
- Survival Notes > Legal Summaries
- Public Trust > Rage

## Interpretation

These principles mean:
- product language should center on red flags and survival guidance
- evidence should be visible, linked, and reviewed
- AI may assist internally but should not decide publication
- product choices should favor maintainability over sophistication
- public trust matters more than outrage or attention hacks
