# Product Overview

## Product Definition

PoliBrawl is a survival guide and community signal board for platform-dependent workers.

It is not:
- a generic ToS summarizer
- a legal advice product
- a raw policy database
- a full crawler
- a forum
- a GRC or enterprise compliance tool

## Target Users

- freelancers
- bug bounty hunters
- creators
- indie hackers
- small online businesses

These users depend on third-party platforms for payout, distribution, infrastructure, identity, or account access. They need practical survival guidance more than clause-by-clause legal summaries.

## Core Platform Categories

- Payment
- Creator/Freelance
- SaaS/Developer

## Core Product Unit

The core product unit is the Red Flag.

A Red Flag is a reviewed, evidence-backed editorial finding about a platform behavior, policy power, restriction, dependency, or failure mode that users should prepare for.

## Platform Survival Page

A Platform Survival Page is built from:
- Red Flags
- Evidence
- Survival Notes
- Backup Options
- Checklist
- Community Signals

## Product Positioning

PoliBrawl should feel like:
- an evidence-first red flag database
- a survival guide for platform-dependent workers
- a community signal board
- a solo-founder-operable editorial product

It should not feel like:
- a policy archive
- an accusation site
- a generic legal dashboard
- a social feed
