# ADR-003: No AI Auto-Publish

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The MVP may benefit from AI-assisted drafting or internal suggestions, but PoliBrawl must remain evidence-first, human-reviewed, and low-risk from an editorial and safety standpoint.

## Decision

AI may assist internal drafts but cannot publish or approve red flags.

## Consequences

- AI outputs are draft material only
- approval and publishing require human review
- dangerous wording, evidence checks, and publish guards stay enforced server-side
- scanner and assistant tooling should not be framed as autonomous editorial authority
