# ADR-002: Structured Community, Not Forum

## Status

Accepted

## Context

PoliBrawl needs useful community input, but the founder operates with limited moderation capacity and the MVP explicitly excludes public comments, forums, and social feeds.

## Decision

Community input is structured signals, not comments, forum, or social feed.

## Consequences

- use forms such as request review, watch platform, submit experience, suggest tip, and correction
- default community submissions to pending
- publish aggregate counts or reviewed anonymized summaries only
- avoid thread, reply, DM, and profile-feed architecture in MVP
