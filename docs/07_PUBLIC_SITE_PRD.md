# Public Site PRD

Epic C will turn the internal CMS output into public PoliBrawl pages.

## Core Public Surfaces

## Landing Page

Purpose: explain the product, show why platform dependence matters, and push users into platform pages or requests.

Must include:
- simple value proposition
- platform category entry points
- featured platforms
- methodology and disclaimer links
- request review CTA

## Platform List

Purpose: browse covered platforms by category and demand.

Must include:
- searchable platform cards
- category filters
- latest reviewed or most requested signals

## Platform Survival Page

Purpose: the main SEO and product page for each platform.

Must include:
- platform summary
- red flags
- evidence
- survival notes
- backup options
- checklist
- community signals
- request review / watch / correction actions

## Red Flag Card

Purpose: show one evidence-backed finding clearly.

Must include:
- title
- category
- level
- summary
- why it matters
- reviewed date

## Evidence Block

Purpose: show short proof linked back to a source.

Must include:
- short excerpt
- source name
- source URL
- reviewed date

## Survival Notes

Purpose: translate the finding into practical actions or precautions.

Must include:
- concise guidance
- plain language
- no legal-advice framing

## Backup Options

Purpose: help users consider alternatives or contingency paths.

Must include:
- option name
- short summary
- tradeoffs

## Checklist

Purpose: provide scannable survival actions.

Must include:
- title
- ordered checklist items

## Community Signals Block

Purpose: show safe aggregate demand and lived-signal data without turning into a forum.

Must include:
- request review count
- watcher count
- safe aggregate experience counts
- approved tip count if available

## Most Requested Leaderboard

Purpose: show which platforms users most want covered or updated.

Must include:
- platform name
- request count
- optional status such as covered or pending

## Methodology Page

Purpose: explain how PoliBrawl reviews sources, publishes red flags, and handles community inputs.

## Disclaimer Page

Purpose: explain independence, trademark usage, editorial nature, and no-legal-advice boundary.

## Out of Scope For This Epic

- public comments
- profile pages
- live discussion threads
- direct messaging
