# Editorial Safety

This document condenses the prior root `CODING_POLICY.md` into the rules that matter for the Red Flag Survival MVP.

## Core Position

PoliBrawl is an independent editorial product. It is not a platform partner, legal service, or public accusation board.

## Brand and Trademark Rules

- no official SaaS logos
- platform names as text only
- no brand cloning
- no favicon scraping
- no official screenshots as decorative UI
- no intentional mimicry of official brand palettes

Use generic or original category icons only.

## Wording Rules

- use neutral wording
- describe what the policy or evidence says
- avoid rage framing
- avoid legal conclusions unless clearly sourced and necessary

Do not use public-facing wording such as:
- scam
- fraud
- theft
- criminal
- evil
- platform stole money

Prefer wording such as:
- the policy describes
- this may allow
- this may affect users who
- community reports mention
- evidence reviewed by PoliBrawl suggests

## Evidence Rules

- evidence required
- every public red flag needs at least one reviewed evidence item
- short excerpt only
- source link required
- reviewed date required

If evidence is missing, keep the item private.

## Disclaimer Rules

Each platform page should clearly state that PoliBrawl is independent and not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by the platform. Public pages should also state that the content is editorial, evidence-based, and not legal advice.

## Community Content Rules

- community content moderation is required
- all community submissions default to pending
- no raw public accusations
- no public comments or replies in MVP
- show approved aggregate signals first

## Alternatives and Backup Options

- alternatives must include tradeoffs
- do not frame backup options as absolute endorsements
- disclose sponsorship or affiliate relationships clearly

## Sponsorship Rules

- sponsorship disclosure required
- sponsors must not influence red flags, evidence, rankings, or survival notes
- sponsored blocks must look distinct from editorial blocks

## Publication Rules

- no defamatory wording
- no publish without evidence
- no publish without reviewed date
- no brand-clone presentation
- no community auto-publish

## Final Standard

PoliBrawl should read like a calm, evidence-first survival guide with public trust in mind. It should never look like a hate site, a legal claim factory, or a platform clone.
